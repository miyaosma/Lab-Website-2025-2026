Fumble is a student club where members support each other in learning. We share notes or trade study tasks in a simple system. Our mission is to make studying easier and effective while keeping academic integrity.

What we do

How Fumble helps students succeed

Fumble lets students exchange help with notes, tutoring, or study tasks. Members offer what they can, set conditions, and others accept trades that fit their needs. It's an easy way to learn together, share skills, and support each other throughout the semester.
